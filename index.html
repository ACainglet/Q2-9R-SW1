Our Products:

Pandesal

Pan De Manila's bread product, Pandesal,
is one of the best served hot yeast bread rolls.
It is puffy, soft, salty and sweet.

Cheesestick Pesto bread

Cheesestick bread, is also one of the best-selling bread products. It is made from flour, yeast, oil, garlic, especially cheese for toppings. It produces this soft, fluffy feeling, and a tasty aroma.

Tasty whole bread

Tasty bread is a type of whole bread made from flour, yeast, olive oil, including fat. It is soft, fluffy, and tasty when touched and eaten.

Whole Wheat bread

Whole Wheat bread is also a type of a whole bread made from whole wheat flour, all components of grains, and healthy fats. It has also a soft and fluffy composition and a tasty flavoring.
